What Ubik does

Ubik is a single workspace that replaces the daily juggle between email, meeting notes, approval chains, project trackers, and dashboards. It reads across your connected tools (Gmail, Google Calendar, Drive, and others you authorize) and surfaces the work that actually needs you.

01 · Inbox

Operator Inbox

A prioritized queue of messages, follow-ups, and approvals — grouped by what's blocking revenue, the plant, or the team today.

02 · Meetings

Meetings

Pre-meeting briefs, live note capture, and post-meeting action items that flow directly into the inbox and project trackers.

03 · Approvals

Approvals

One queue for purchase, packaging, compliance, and finance approvals — with context pulled from the originating thread.

04 · Execution

Projects & Workflows

Lightweight projects and repeatable workflows that wire up the recurring operator motions — RFQs, order tracking, renewals, audits.

05 · Intelligence

Know Anything

Ask anything about your operation. Ubik pulls from your org knowledge, meetings, and files to answer — with citations.

06 · Agents

Agents

Assign repeatable tasks to Ubik agents — draft replies, prep briefs, chase follow-ups, summarize threads — under your review.

How it works

  1. Step 01

    Sign in

    Sign in with Google or Microsoft. Ubik asks only for the scopes it needs to read and draft work for you — you stay in control.

  2. Step 02

    Connect your stack

    Authorize the tools your team already runs on — email, calendar, documents. Ubik reads the context it needs; it does not move or copy data outside your workspace.

  3. Step 03

    Run the day

    Ubik surfaces what needs you, drafts what it can, and waits for your call on the rest. Every action is logged; nothing ships without your sign-off.

Security & data use

Ubik only requests the Google and Microsoft OAuth scopes required to provide the features you enable. We do not sell your data, we do not use it to train third-party models, and you can revoke access at any time from your Google or Microsoft account settings.

For the full details on what we collect, why, and how long we keep it, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
